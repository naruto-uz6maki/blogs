places must visit

Reno,Nevada

i had visited in 2019

Reno is a smaller city which is growing pretty fast. It has a lot of character, grungy, artsy, with great outdoor activities. Traffic is getting more busy as more people are moving in. It offers beautiful skies, mountains, wildlife. Summers are hot and dry, winters are moderate to cold with occasional snow. There farmers markets in summer. It has all the necessary shopping needs, however not a huge variety like in a large city. Reno is also known for its casinos. There are lots of dining opportunities, with many sushi options. Some city areas are being remodeled and gentrified. Some areas I would say safer than others, like in every city. There are a couple good ares for off-leash dog play, a community college and a university.
